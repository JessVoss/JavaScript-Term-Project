This site is geared towards everyone.

The Gray wolf was once an animal people feared. They once ranged a
great part of the Northern Hemisphere. They currently range in only parts of North America, areas within some European countries, and select ranges in Asia.

In recent years the numbers have gone up and the wolves have been taken off the
endangered list. It is now legal to hunt and trap again.
